{{ YOUR NAME / COMPANY NAME }} ("we", "us") operates the mobile application **Tile Flip** (the "App"). This page explains what data the App processes and how.

## 1. Data we collect directly

**We do not collect or transmit any personal information ourselves.** The App stores the following entirely on your device using Android / iOS local storage (`shared_preferences`):

- Your best move count and star rating per level
- Your Infinite mode streak and best-ever streak
- Total wins (used only to time interstitial ads)
- Whether you have unlocked each level

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App erases it.

## 2. Data collected by third-party SDKs

The App shows ads via **Google AdMob** (Google LLC). AdMob and any mediation partners may collect:

- Your device's **Advertising ID** (resettable in system settings)
- Coarse location (country/region) inferred from IP
- Device model, OS version, screen size, language
- App open/close timestamps
- Interaction with ads (impressions, clicks)

Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. Consent for personalised advertising (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, when you first open the App you will see a Google-provided consent form (via the **User Messaging Platform, "UMP"**) asking whether you agree to:

- Personalised ads (your Advertising ID may be shared with advertising partners)
- Non-personalised ads only (Advertising ID still used for frequency capping and basic measurement)
- Refuse consent entirely (the App will attempt to serve non-personalised ads or none at all)

You can change your choice any time: **Settings → Privacy choices** inside the App.

## 4. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the **GDPR** (EEA/UK) or **CCPA/CPRA** (California):

- **Access** — request a copy of the data an advertising partner holds about you.
- **Deletion** — request deletion of that data.
- **Opt-out of personalised advertising / sale of personal information** — use the in-app Privacy choices flow above, **or** reset/limit your Advertising ID in system settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

To exercise these rights against Google's AdMob data, contact Google directly via the controls at [https://adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com). For any questions about the App itself, contact us at **{{ contact email, e.g. you@yourdomain.example }}**.

The App is rated for a general audience (PEGI 3 / Everyone). It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used the App, contact us and we will ensure no related data is retained by our advertising partners.
