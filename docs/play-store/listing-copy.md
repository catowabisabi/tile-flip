## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)

> A minimal tap-puzzle in your pocket.
>
> Every tile has two states — light or dark. Tap one and it flips, along with its four orthogonal neighbours. Turn the whole board to a single shade to win.
>
> **1000 hand-tuned puzzles** — grids grow from 4×4 warmups to 7×7 brain-melters. Every puzzle is generated from a fixed seed, so the challenge is identical on every device. Beat par, earn up to three coral stars per level.
>
> **Infinite mode** — a never-ending stream of boards, scaling in difficulty the longer you play. How long can you keep your streak alive?
>
> **Built for calm play** — no timers, no energy meters, no push notifications. Just you and the grid.
>
> **Designed for accessibility** — high-contrast dark theme, clear visual feedback, works with one thumb.
>
> **Privacy first** — all progress is stored on your device. The only data collected is what ad networks require (Advertising ID). You can review and change your ad privacy choices any time from Settings → Privacy choices.
>
> Free to play, supported by ads.
